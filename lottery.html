The Bryant, Hunts Point

The Bryant offers an array of amenities suitable for families and individuals, including a Children’s activity room, Multi-purpose room, Computer room, Library, Lounge, in-building Laundry, Bike storage, and on-site social services tailored for low-income or formerly homeless households with special needs. Additional conveniences include a front desk attendant and a live-in Super, enhancing the security and maintenance of the building. Located in Hunts Point, the residence benefits from excellent public transit accessibility, including proximity to the 2, 5, & 6 trains and several bus lines. Tenants will be responsible for electricity, including an electric stove. More details are available at NYHousingSearch.gov.

Tree of Life, Jamaica

Tree of Life stands as a testament to sustainable living, being a 174-unit affordable housing development constructed to Passive House standards. It prioritizes energy efficiency and resident comfort through increased insulation, high-performance windows, and innovative heating and cooling systems. The building boasts amenities such as a fitness room, multipurpose room, outdoor green spaces, bike storage, laundry facilities on every floor, and covered parking with electric car charging stations. Located in downtown Jamaica, it offers easy access to local transportation options.

Bedford Green House, Bedford Park

Bedford Green House presents 46 newly constructed units featuring comprehensive amenities, including a community room, bicycle storage, laundry facilities, a 24-hour front desk concierge, rooftop terrace, green roof, and playground. The building is transit-friendly, located near multiple bus and train options. It promotes a smoke-free living environment, with no application or broker’s fees required.

Bishop Philius and Helene Nicolas (BPHN) Senior Residence, East Flatbush

BPHN Senior Residence addresses the demand for affordable senior housing in East Flatbush. The 7-story building showcases a modern terra cotta finish and energy-efficient design. It includes 89 units of senior housing alongside social service spaces and a community senior center managed by Evangelical Crusade Christian Church, benefiting both the residents and the local community. Eligible tenants will pay 30% of their income, with Section 8 qualification required.
