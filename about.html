Our Company

Springer Capital Properties is a full-service firm specializing in the development, construction, and management of high-quality affordable housing. For more than 75 years, Springer Capital Properties has invested in the growth and sustainability of New York City’s neighborhoods through a long-term ownership strategy focused on successful partnerships with government, community stakeholders, and residents. The firm has developed or preserved thousands of affordable apartments and today manages over 6,000 units across the city.

Through Springer Capital Properties Development Company LLC, the company acquires property, assembles and manages development teams, and arranges financing for new projects. Springer Capital Properties Construction Corp. is the firm’s general contracting company with a track record of successfully completing multifamily renovations and new construction projects. SCP also contracts with outside clients. Springer Capital Properties, Inc. is the firm’s property management business that manages property for its own portfolio and for nonprofit or for-profit building owners. The company has a team of dedicated professionals with years of management experience and skills in rules and regulations covering affordable housing programs.

Investment Criteria

  • Primarily limited partner equity
  • One-off investments rather than fund structures
  • Target equity ranges from $2M to $20M USD
  • Typical 3 - 5 year holding periods
  • Focus on high growth cities

Strategic Housing Solutions & Partnerships

Springer Capital Properties continues to expand its impact in the affordable housing sector while also delivering market-rate housing, mixed-income developments, and supportive housing throughout the city. The company’s leadership, reputation, and industry know-how make Springer Capital Properties an ideal partner for a variety of projects, including those with challenging development, construction, property management, or financial conditions.
